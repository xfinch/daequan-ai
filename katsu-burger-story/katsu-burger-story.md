# The Bonsai Entrepreneur: A Visit to Katsu Burger

**Date:** May 18, 2026  
**Location:** Katsu Burger (CB Mobile Client)  
**Entrepreneur:** Christopher — Fifth-Generation Bonsai Artist, Junior Master

## The Philosophy

Christopher doesn't just open restaurants. He cultivates them.

As a fifth-generation bonsai artist and junior master, he approaches entrepreneurship with the same patience and vision his family has applied to trees for generations. The process is strikingly similar:

1. **Find the raw material** — a space with potential, a person with fire
2. **Shape with intention** — soft launch, mentor, build systems
3. **Let it stand on its own** — hand ownership to the person who earned it
4. **Move to the next seedling** — repeat under the Katsu Burger name

## The Business Model

Christopher's approach inverts the traditional franchise playbook:

- **Find the space** — Identify a location with promise
- **Soft launch** — Test the concept, build local traction
- **Find the enthusiast** — Identify the person most passionate about the vision
- **Raise them up** — Train, mentor, equip them to succeed
- **Make them the owner** — Transfer ownership, step back
- **Move on** — Find the next opportunity

Like his ancestors walking through forests and nurseries, spotting potential in unremarkable trees, Christopher sees future restaurateurs before they see themselves.

## Why CB Mobile Fits

Christopher chose Stephen Mobile because it aligns with his philosophy:

- **Lower overhead** — More resources to invest in people, not infrastructure
- **Flexibility** — The agility to test, iterate, and grow organically
- **Motivation-friendly** — Systems that empower rather than constrain

The partnership allows him to keep expenses minimal while maximizing his ability to develop the next generation of Katsu Burger operators.

## The Parallel

Bonsai isn't about controlling growth — it's about *directing* it. Christopher applies this same wisdom to business:

> Just as he was passed down the art and craft of bonsai, he passes down the hard craft of entrepreneurship.

Each Katsu Burger location is a tree he's shaped. Each operator is an artist he's trained. And when they're ready, he does what every good bonsai master does:

He lets them grow.
