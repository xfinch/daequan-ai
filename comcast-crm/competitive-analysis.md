## Comcast Positioning Strengths

1. **Speed-to-Install (HFC)** — 30-60 days beats AT&T's 120 days for non-fiber-ready locations
2. **Speed Capacity** — 400G fiber exceeds most competitors (except AT&T's 1 Tbps overkill)
3. **Threat Reporting** — Available when Spectrum/Frontier don't offer it
4. **Reliability** — 99.99% matches best-in-class

## Comcast Vulnerabilities

1. **Wireless Backup** — Not available until 2026 (AT&T/Verizon/Spectrum have it now)
2. **Contract Flexibility** — Locked 3-year vs Lumen's 1-5 year options
3. **Fiber Install Speed** — 120 days vs AT&T's 10-20 days for ready locations

---

## Offer Derivation Strategies

### Against AT&T
- **Lead with HFC speed-to-install** if location not fiber-ready
- **Counter 1 Tbps** with "right-sizing" — most businesses don't need petabyte-scale
- **Emphasize 99.99% reliability** vs their 99.95%

### Against Lumen
- **Counter contract flexibility** with Comcast's install speed and reliability
- **Lumen has no wireless backup** — neutralizes that Comcast gap

### Against Spectrum/Frontier
- **Threat reporting advantage** — security-conscious businesses
- **Speed capacity** — 400G vs their 100 Gbps

### Against Verizon
- **Speed capacity** — 400G matches their 100 Gbps offering
- **Transparency** — Verizon undisclosed on several key metrics
