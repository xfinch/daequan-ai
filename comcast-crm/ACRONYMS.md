# Comcast Acronyms & Definitions

Quick reference for Comcast business terms, products, and internal shorthand.

## Products & Services

| Acronym | Full Name | Description |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| BVE | Business Voice Edge | Comcast's cloud-based business phone system (VoIP) |
| BBE | Business Broadband Edge | High-speed internet for business (if different from residential) |
| TV | Television | Business TV packages |
| HSI | High-Speed Internet | Standard internet service |

## Sales & Pipeline Terms

| Acronym | Full Name | Description |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| DM | Decision Maker | Person with authority to purchase/approve |
| BAE | Business Account Executive | Sales role (your title) |
| B2B | Business-to-Business | Selling to other businesses |
| SMB | Small-Medium Business | Target customer segment |

## Territory & Account Types

| Term | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Prospect | Potential customer not yet signed |
| Gatekeeper | Person who controls access to DM |
| Follow-up | Needs additional contact/action |
| Interested | Engaged, potential to close |
| Customer | Already signed with Comcast |
