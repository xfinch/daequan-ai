## Project Scope

**Purpose:** Filter serious buyers/sellers from casual inquiries  
**Integration:** HomeGroup.com website → GHL → Qualification Tool  

---

## How It Works

**Current Problem:**
- HomeGroup.com generates real estate leads
- Too many unqualified/tire-kicker leads
- Wastes agent time on follow-ups
- Hard to identify serious prospects

**Solution:**
- Automated qualification system
- Multi-step filtering process
- Scoring/ranking of lead quality
- Only hot leads get human follow-up

**The "TTL Method":**
1. Capture lead from website
2. Automated qualification sequence (SMS/email)
3. Behavioral tracking (opens, clicks, responses)
4. AI scoring based on engagement + data
5. Hot leads → Agent immediate follow-up
6. Warm leads → Nurture sequence
7. Cold leads → Long-term drip
