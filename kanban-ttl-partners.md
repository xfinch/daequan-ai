## Partner Qualification Criteria

**Good TTL Partners:**
- Work with small-to-mid-size businesses ($500K-$5M revenue)
- Have access to decision-makers with budget authority
- See businesses struggling with growth/operations
- Want to add value to their network (not just extract)

**Red Flags:**
- Only connects to businesses <$100K (can't afford TTL)
- Wants referral fees upfront (not a true partner)
- No actual relationship with prospects (cold leads)
